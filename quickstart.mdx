---
title: 'Quickstart'
description: 'Get live-data into your Google Sheets'
---


<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
  Head over to [Viasheets](https://viasheets.com/sign-up) to create an account, and follow the onboarding steps (Create an Organization and Connect your Sources)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install Google Marketplace Extension">
  Go to the [Google Marketplace](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/search/Viasheets) and install the **Viasheets** app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a Source">
  If you haven't already, go to the [Viasheets Dashboard](https://dash.viasheets.com/dashboard) and connect a source.

  <Info>
  To learn how to connect a source, click [here](/sources/create).
  </Info>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Create & Run a Query">
  Create a Google Sheet (or use one of our existing [templates](/templates)) and create a query. 

  <Info>
  To learn how to create a query, click [here](/query/create). 
  </Info>

  Once you've created a query, run the query.

  <Info>
  To learn how to run a query, click [here](/query/view-and-run). 
  </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>
